01MON

Monday

A strong, energizing start to the week

☀️ Breakfast
Moong Dal Chilla + Mint Chutney
Thin savory pancakes made from soaked moong dal batter. High protein, fiber-rich and super filling. Pairs perfectly with fresh mint-coriander chutney.
High ProteinFiber Rich~25 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Soak 1 cup moong dal overnight. Grind with green chilli, ginger, salt into smooth batter.

2

Heat a non-stick tawa, pour ladle of batter, spread thin like a dosa. Drizzle oil on sides.

3

Cook 2 min each side till golden. Serve with ready-made or fresh mint chutney.

🌤 Lunch
Roti + Dal + Soya Chunks Sabji + Salad
Classic Indian thali — 2 rotis with protein-packed dal, soya chunks curry, and a fresh cucumber-tomato salad. Balanced, filling and wholesome.
BalancedProtein Packed~40 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Pressure cook toor dal with turmeric. Temper with mustard, cumin, onion, tomato, coriander.

2

Soak soya chunks in hot water 10 min, squeeze dry. Cook in onion-tomato masala with spices.

3

Make 2 fresh rotis. Chop cucumber, tomato, onion with lemon and salt for salad.

🌙 Dinner
Paneer Tikka + Salad
Marinated paneer cubes grilled on tawa — smoky, high protein and delicious. Served with a light cucumber-onion salad. Light yet satisfying dinner.
High ProteinGrilled~30 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Mix curd, red chilli powder, garam masala, turmeric, chaat masala, lemon juice. Marinate paneer cubes 20 min.

2

Heat tawa or grill pan with a little oil. Cook paneer on all sides till charred marks appear.

3

Serve with sliced onion, tomato, cucumber and green chutney.

02TUE

Tuesday

Light and nourishing mid-week fuel

☀️ Breakfast
Paneer Stuffed Besan Chilla
Savory gram flour pancakes stuffed with spiced paneer filling. Protein-packed and delicious — kids and adults love it. Quick to make in batches.
Protein Packed~20 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Mix 1 cup besan with water, ajwain, salt, red chilli, coriander leaves to a smooth pourable batter.

2

Grate paneer, mix with salt, green chilli, coriander. Keep aside as stuffing.

3

Pour batter on tawa, spread thin. Add paneer stuffing in center, fold over. Cook both sides golden.

🌤 Lunch
Brown Rice + Rajma + Greek Yogurt + Salad
Hearty rajma with nutty brown rice — a comfort classic. Greek yogurt on the side adds cooling protein. Fresh salad completes the balanced plate.
ComfortingHigh Protein~45 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Soak rajma overnight. Pressure cook 4–5 whistles. Make gravy with onion-tomato masala, add cooked rajma.

2

Cook brown rice with 1:2.5 ratio water. Takes about 30 min — start early.

3

Serve with 2 tbsp Greek yogurt and fresh kachumber salad (onion, tomato, cucumber, lemon).

🌙 Dinner
Grilled Tofu + Vegetable Soup
Light, warm and protein-rich dinner. Tofu grilled with soy sauce and spices alongside a clear vegetable broth. Perfect for a relaxed evening.
LightWarm~30 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Press tofu dry. Marinate with soy sauce, chilli flakes, garlic powder 10 min. Grill on pan with oil.

2

Boil chopped carrots, beans, peas, celery in vegetable stock. Season with pepper and herbs.

3

Serve soup hot with grilled tofu slices on side.

03WED

Wednesday

Wholesome midweek reset

☀️ Breakfast
Vegetable Poha + Peanuts
Flattened rice cooked with mustard, curry leaves, turmeric, onion, peas and crunchy peanuts. Light, tasty with good carbs — a classic Indian breakfast.
LightGood Carbs~20 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Rinse thick poha, let it sit 5 min to soften. Do NOT over-soak.

2

Temper mustard, curry leaves, green chilli in oil. Add onion, fry. Add turmeric, peas, salt.

3

Add poha, mix gently. Top with roasted peanuts, lemon juice and coriander. Serve warm.

🌤 Lunch
Rotis + Paneer Bhurji
Spiced scrambled paneer cooked with onions, tomatoes and peppers. High protein, simple and delicious with fresh rotis. A desi classic that never fails.
High ProteinSimple~30 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Heat oil, sauté finely chopped onion till golden. Add ginger-garlic paste, chopped tomato.

2

Add turmeric, red chilli, coriander powder. Crumble paneer in, mix well on high heat 3 min.

3

Finish with garam masala, coriander leaves. Serve with 2–3 hot rotis.

🌙 Dinner
Palak Tofu Bowl
Creamy spinach curry base with soft tofu cubes — iron rich, satisfying and nutritious. Served over a small portion of rice or eaten as a bowl.
Iron RichCreamy~35 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Blanch 2 cups spinach 1 min in hot water. Blend with green chilli and garlic to smooth puree.

2

Sauté onion, ginger in oil. Add spinach puree, cumin, garam masala, cream/curd. Simmer 10 min.

3

Add cubed tofu gently. Do not over-stir. Serve in bowl over ½ cup plain rice.

04THU

Thursday

Power through the second half

☀️ Breakfast
Idli + Sambar + Coconut Chutney
Steamed rice-lentil cakes with spiced vegetable lentil broth and fresh coconut chutney. Light, fermented and gut-healthy. Great for digestion.
Gut HealthyFermented~20 min (batter ready)

Cook's Instructions

1

Use store-bought or pre-made idli batter. Pour in greased idli moulds, steam 12–15 min.

2

Make sambar with toor dal, tamarind, sambar powder and vegetables (drumstick, tomato, onion).

3

Use ready coconut chutney packet or grind coconut, green chilli, ginger with tempered mustard.

🌤 Lunch
Palak Paneer + 1 Roti
The iconic spinach-paneer curry. Iron rich and protein packed. Just one roti keeps it light without sacrificing satisfaction. A perennial favourite.
Iron RichProtein Packed~35 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Blanch spinach, blend smooth. Sauté onion, ginger-garlic, tomato with whole spices.

2

Add spinach puree, curd, kasuri methi, garam masala. Simmer 8 min.

3

Add paneer cubes (can lightly fry first). Serve with 1 roti and small side of onion.

🌙 Dinner
Moong Dal Bowl + 1 Roti
Simple yellow moong dal with ghee tadka — simple, balanced and wholesome. Pair with 1 roti for a satisfying light dinner that's easy on digestion.
SimpleBalanced~25 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Pressure cook split moong dal with turmeric and salt (2 whistles). Mash slightly.

2

Temper in ghee: cumin seeds, dried red chilli, asafoetida. Pour over dal.

3

Serve in bowl with 1 roti and a drizzle of lemon juice on dal.

05FRI

Friday

Wind down into the weekend — keep it flavorful

☀️ Breakfast
Upma with Lots of Vegetables
Semolina cooked with mustard, curry leaves, onion, carrots, beans, peas and green chilli. Warm, comforting and wholesome. A fill-you-up morning classic.
WarmComforting~20 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Dry roast 1 cup rava/semolina in pan 3–4 min till fragrant. Keep aside.

2

Temper mustard, curry leaves, chilli in oil. Add onion, vegetables. Fry 3 min.

3

Add 2.5 cups water, salt, bring to boil. Add roasted rava stirring continuously. Cook till thick. Garnish with coriander and lemon.

🌤 Lunch
Vegetable Paneer Pulao + Raita
Flavourful one-pot pulao with paneer and vegetables. Serve with a simple curd-based raita. Balanced, aromatic and satisfying — feels like a treat.
FlavorfulOne-Pot~40 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Fry whole spices in ghee. Add onion, ginger-garlic. Add diced vegetables, paneer, sauté 5 min.

2

Add soaked basmati rice, water (1:1.75), salt. Cover and cook on low till rice is done.

3

Make raita: whisk curd with salt, cumin powder, grated cucumber or boondi.

🌙 Dinner
Soya Chunks Curry + Rice
Protein-rich soya chunks in a hearty masala gravy with steamed rice. Satisfying, filling and budget-friendly. Tastes better when the gravy simmers long.
Protein RichHearty~35 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Boil soya chunks 5 min, squeeze and drain. Makes them absorb masala better.

2

Make thick onion-tomato-cashew gravy with standard masalas. Add soya chunks, simmer 15 min.

3

Steam ½ cup rice. Serve curry generously over rice with a wedge of lemon.

06SAT

Saturday

Weekend variety — a bit more special

☀️ Breakfast
Ragi Dosa + Paneer Filling
Crispy finger millet dosas filled with spiced paneer. High fiber, iron-rich and a great weekend breakfast. Nutritious and deliciously different.
High FiberIron Rich~30 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Mix ragi flour with rice flour, curd, salt, water to thin batter consistency. Rest 10 min.

2

Make paneer filling: crumbled paneer + chopped onion + green chilli + coriander + salt.

3

Pour batter thin on hot tawa. Place paneer filling on one side, fold dosa over. Serve with coconut chutney.

🌤 Lunch
Chole Salad Bowl
Protein-packed chickpeas tossed with onion, tomato, cucumber, lemon, chaat masala and fresh herbs. Light, refreshing and incredibly nutritious.
LightNo Cook~15 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Use canned or pre-cooked/soaked chickpeas. Drain and rinse well.

2

Chop onion, tomato, cucumber, coriander. Mix with chickpeas.

3

Add lemon juice, chaat masala, black salt, green chilli. Toss well and serve fresh.

🌙 Dinner
Stir-Fried Broccoli + Paneer
High protein, detoxifying stir fry with tender broccoli florets and golden paneer. Tossed with garlic, soy sauce and mild spices. Quick weeknight favourite.
DetoxifyingHigh Protein~20 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Blanch broccoli florets 2 min. Cut paneer in 1-inch cubes. Chop garlic and spring onion.

2

Heat wok, add oil. Fry paneer till golden, set aside. Stir-fry garlic, add broccoli on high heat.

3

Add soy sauce, chilli flakes, salt, pepper. Return paneer to pan. Toss and serve immediately.

07SUN

Sunday

Relax and reset for the week ahead

☀️ Breakfast
Oats Cheela with Veggies
High-fiber oats pancakes loaded with veggies — a nutritious, filling and healthy breakfast. High fiber, keeps you full through a lazy Sunday morning.
High FiberFilling~20 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Blend rolled oats to coarse flour. Mix with besan (½:½), curd, water, spices into batter.

2

Fold in grated carrot, chopped spinach, onion, green chilli.

3

Cook on non-stick tawa with minimal oil till both sides are golden and crisp. Serve with curd or chutney.

🌤 Lunch
Dal Khichdi + Greek Yogurt
One-pot rice and lentil comfort food with ghee tadka. Easy to digest, warm and wholesome. The ultimate Sunday reset meal. Greek yogurt cools it down perfectly.
Easy to DigestWholesome~30 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Wash and combine rice + moong dal (1:1). Add turmeric, salt, 4 cups water. Pressure cook 3 whistles.

2

Make tadka: ghee, cumin, hing, dried red chilli, ginger julienne. Pour over khichdi.

3

Serve hot with 2 tbsp Greek yogurt and papad on side. Add pickle if desired.

🌙 Dinner
Light Khichdi + Curd
A lighter, plain khichdi for Sunday dinner — easy on the stomach, minimal effort and deeply nourishing. Curd on the side aids digestion overnight.
LightMinimal Effort~20 min

Cook's Instructions

1

Cook plain khichdi: rice + moong dal + salt + turmeric + water in cooker. Keep it soft/mushy.

2

Top with a spoon of ghee and simple cumin-pepper tadka.

3

Serve in bowl with chilled curd and a pinch of jeera powder on curd. Simple, comforting and done.

🧑‍🍳 Cook's Cheat Sheet — Instructions for Your Maid/Cook

📦

Weekly groceries to keep stocked: Paneer (500g), Tofu (1 block), Moong dal, Toor dal, Soya chunks, Brown rice, Basmati rice, Besan, Oats, Ragi flour, Curd/Greek Yogurt, seasonal vegetables (broccoli, spinach, capsicum, carrots, peas, onion, tomato), lemons.

🧊

Prep ahead: Soak rajma and chickpeas overnight whenever needed. Keep moong dal batter ready in fridge for 2 days. Pre-cook brown rice in bulk and refrigerate — reheat with a splash of water.

⏱️

Time saving: Make a big batch of onion-tomato masala base twice a week — it works for rajma, soya, paneer and dal curries. Refrigerate in a container.

🧂

Keep these ready: Readymade chaat masala, garam masala, sambar powder and coconut chutney packets reduce complexity without affecting taste. Totally acceptable shortcuts.

🥗

Salad every lunch: Just chop cucumber, tomato, onion with lemon and salt. Takes 3 minutes. Do it while other dishes cook.

🔄

If something is unavailable: Paneer and tofu are interchangeable in most recipes. Brown rice can be swapped for white basmati. Any dal can be replaced with moong dal which cooks fastest.
